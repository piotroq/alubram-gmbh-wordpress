## 1. Project Overview

### 1.1 Project Name

**ALUBRAM GMBH WordPress Website**

### 1.2 Project Type

Premium WordPress website development for Austrian aluminum products manufacturer.

### 1.3 Project Summary

ALUBRAM GMBH requires a modern, high-performance WordPress website to showcase their premium aluminum products (fences, gates, carports, canopies) to the DACH market (Austria, Germany, Switzerland). The website must reflect the company's premium positioning, generate qualified leads, and provide comprehensive product information in German.

### 1.4 Project Duration

**12-16 weeks** from kickoff to launch.

## 10. Risks & Mitigation

### 10.1 Identified Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| **Content Delays** | Medium | High | Early content brief, template content ready |
| **Plugin Conflicts** | Low | Medium | Staging testing, backup before updates |
| **Performance Issues** | Low | High | Early testing, optimization budget |
| **DSGVO Non-Compliance** | Medium | Critical | Legal review, Borlabs Cookie plugin |
| **Hosting Issues** | Low | High | Reliable host, backups, monitoring |
| **Scope Creep** | Medium | Medium | Clear requirements, change request process |
| **Timeline Delays** | Medium | Medium | Buffer time built in, early communication |

### 10.2 Contingency Plans

**Content Delays:**
- Template content prepared in advance
- Client provided with content guidelines early
- Parallel content creation (design/dev can proceed with placeholders)

**Performance Issues:**
- Performance budget defined (LCP < 2.5s, etc.)
- Early testing on staging
- Optimization techniques ready (image compression, caching)

**DSGVO Issues:**
- Borlabs Cookie plugin pre-configured
- Datenschutzerklärung template from lawyer
- Google Fonts hosted locally
- Maps behind cookie consent
